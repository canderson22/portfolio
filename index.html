About Me

Hi! My name is Charles Anderson. I am a JavaScript Full Stack Developer working with the latest technologies. I am currently working with React, Redux, Node, Express, and MongoDB. I also have experience working with Ruby on Rails, jQuery, Bootstrap, Materialize, and Socket.io.

Prior to becoming a Web Developer, I interned at a nonprofit organization focused on Justice Reform.
As an intern, I was responsible for managing charity events, mailing systems, and raising awareness around the organization and its programs, which allowed me to refine and strengthen my managerial and communication skills.

Currently, I am working on a web app called General Assembly Shop, built with React, Redux, Node, Express, and MongoDB, where users can buy General Assembly apparel, chat with other users, and view future GA events.
